## 👨‍💻 About Me

I’m an engineering student at **CentraleSupélec (’27)** with a rigorous foundation in mathematics and physics (MPSI/MP*). My focus lies at the intersection of **Statistical Machine Learning, Deep Learning Theory, and ML Systems**. 

Instead of treating AI as a black box, I am passionate about understanding low-level model optimization, statistical feature engineering, and theoretical interpretability.

- 🧠 I enjoy building ML architectures from scratch to deeply understand their mechanics (e.g., Transformers, attention complexity).
- 🔭 I lead AI R&D at **[ZenkoLab](https://www.zenkolab.dev)**, focusing on robust empirical Deep Learning (ViT fine-tuning) and interpretability on highly noisy clinical datasets.
- 📈 Currently competing in the **ENS x QRT Data Challenge** (Time-Series & Asset Allocation Forecasting).
- 📍 Currently completing a semester at **ESSEC Singapore** 🇸🇬.
